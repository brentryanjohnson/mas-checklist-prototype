## Clauses mandating compliance within set timeframes

  <div class="usa-alert usa-alert--warning">
    <div class="usa-alert__body">
      <h3 class="usa-alert__heading">These actions are required.</h3>
    </div>
  </div>
<table class="usa-table usa-table--borderless">
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th scope="col">Action</th>
      <th scope="col">Complete by</th>
      <th scope="col">Clause</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        <p>Report schedule contract sales using the <a href="https://srp.fas.gsa.gov/">FAS Sales Reporting Portal</a>.</p> 
        <p><strong>Report each quarter</strong> if your contract is non-TDR.</p>
        <p><strong>Report monthly</strong> if your contract is TDR.</p>
      </td>
      <td>
        30 days following completion of each reporting quarter/month
      </td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/gsam/552.238-80">552.238-80</a><br>
          Industrial Funding Fee and Sales Reporting
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        Report all price reductions subject to this clause to your contracting officer.
        </td>
      <td>15 days after effective date of price reduction</td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/content/552.238-81-price-reductions.">552.238-81</a><br> 
        Price Reductions
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        When applicable, report first-tier subcontract information and executive compensation via the <a href="http://www.fsrs.gov">Federal Funding Accountability and Transparency Act Subaward Reporting System</a> (FSRS).
        </td>
      <td>See clause</td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/far/52.204-10">52.204-10</a><br> 
        Reporting Executive Compensation and First-Tier Subcontract Awards
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        Update information in the Federal Awardee Performance and Integrity Information System (FAPIIS) by posting the required information in the SAM database.
        </td>
      <td>Semi-annually throughout the life of this contract</td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/far/52.209-9">52.209-9</a><br> 
        Updates of Publicly Available Information Regarding Responsibility Matters
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        Communicate CAGE code changes to the Contracting Officer. Contractors undergoing a novation or change-of-name agreement shall notify the contracting officer in accordance with subpart <a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/far/subpart-42.12">42.12</a> Novation and Change-of-Name Agreements.
        </td>
      <td>30 days after the change</td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/far/52.204-18">52.204-18</a><br> 
        Commercial and Government Entity Code Maintenance
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        Maintain a current, accurate and complete <a href="https://sam.gov/SAM/">SAM.gov</a> profile and have an active registration in SAM through final payment of this contract.
      </td>
      <td>Annually</td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/far/52.204-7">52.204-7</a><br> 
        System for Award Management<br>
        <a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/gsam/552.212-4">552.212-4</a><br>
        Contract Terms and Conditions—Commercial Products and Commercial Services (FAR DEVIATION)
      </td>
    </tr> 
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        Submit information regarding services performed during the previous Government fiscal year via <a href="https://sam.gov/SAM/">SAM.gov</a>.
      </td>
      <td>Annually by October 31</td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/far/52.204-15">52.204-15</a><br> 
        Service Contract Reporting Requirements for Indefinite-Delivery Contracts
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        Provide written notice to the Contracting Officer within five (5) business days of becoming an inverted domestic corporation or a subsidiary of an inverted domestic corporation.
      </td>
      <td>Five (5) business days</td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/far/52.209-10">52.209-10</a><br> 
        Prohibition on Contracting with Inverted Domestic Corporations
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        If the business size changed, re-represent business size in <a href="https://sam.gov/">SAM.gov</a> prior to option exercise and within a specified period of time after executing a novation agreement or merger/acquisition that does not require a novation.
      </td>
      <td>See clause</td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/far/52.219-28">52.219-28</a><br> 
        Post-Award Small Business Program Rerepresentation
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        “Other than small” contractors shall submit reports documenting subcontracting performance under this contract to the <a href="https://www.esrs.gov/">Electronic Subcontracting Reporting System (ESRS)</a>.
      </td>
      <td>See clause</td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/far/52.219-9">52.219-9</a><br> 
        Small Business Subcontracting Plan
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td scope="row">
        Provide written notice to the Contracting Officer within five (5) days of initiating bankruptcy proceedings.
      </td>
      <td>Five (5) days</td>
      <td><a href="https://www.acquisition.gov/far/52.242-13">52.242-13</a><br> 
        Bankruptcy
      </td>
    </tr>                
  </tbody>
</table>

If ambiguities arise between this document and the terms and conditions of your MAS contract, the terms and conditions incorporated into your MAS contract shall prevail._
